About IPFS

Remember you can upload your bitmosaic text file to IPFS, that allows you to save a copy of your bitmosaic in a distributed file system.

You can use https://pinata.cloud to upload your bitmosaic text files.

Keep your recovery information in a safe place and never share it.

You can learn more about IPFS at https://ipfs.io
